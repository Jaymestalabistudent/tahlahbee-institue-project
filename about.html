Our | Origin. Story. Start.

Tahlahbee, the visionary founder of Tahlahbee Institute, is a devoted father who has navigated the challenges of family life, from marriage breakdowns to court proceedings and periods of absence from his children.

Realizing the lack of support for single fathers, he founded the Tahlahbee Institute in 2022 to provide practical advice, eBooks, and a supportive network. The Institute quickly grew into a vital resource for single dads. That same year, Tahlahbee launched a video campaign with IC7 Clothing to raise awareness and celebrate the resilience of single fathers.
Join us at Tahlahbee Institute, where we empower fathers to thrive.

Early 2022

Tahlahbee, after experiencing the struggles of being a single father firsthand, identifies the need for a resource dedicated to supporting single dads. He begins to conceptualize the Tahlahbee Institute, envisioning a platform offering eBooks, advice, and connections to other single fathers.

Mid 2022

Tahlahbee conducts extensive research to understand the specific needs and challenges faced by single fathers. He begins to outline the structure and content of the institute, focusing on creating valuable resources that provide practical and actionable advice.

Late 2022

Tahlahbee starts developing the core content for the institute. This includes writing eBooks, creating advice courses, and compiling inspirational quotes. He also reaches out to other single fathers to gather their input and experiences, ensuring the content is relevant and helpful.

Early 2023

The official Tahlahbee Institute Instagram under the name 4rmendz is launched. Tahlahbee begins producing content for social media campaigns. The initial response is positive, with many great reviews.

Late 2023

Tahlahbee Institute begins to research eBooks, advice courses, and interactive features such as forums and live Q&A sessions. Tahlahbee works on building a strong community of single fathers, encouraging them to share their experiences and support one another.

Mid 2024

Tahlahbee Institute pre-launches their website. The platform's user base grows significantly, and it becomes a trusted source of support for single fathers. Tahlahbee continues to evolve the institute, adding new features and resources based on user feedback and emerging needs.

Tahlahbee Institute, born out of Tahlahbee's personal struggles and determination, has grown from an idea in 2022 into a vital resource for single fathers. Through careful planning, community engagement, and ongoing development, it has established itself as a supportive and empowering platform. As it continues to evolve, Tahlahbee Institute remains dedicated to helping single fathers navigate their challenges and find strength in their journey.
