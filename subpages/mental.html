Mental health affects everyone below are a few links to services 

Psychology Today : Psychology Today is a comprehensive online resource dedicated to providing information and insights on mental health, psychology, and human behavior. The website features a wide array of articles, expert opinions, and interactive tools designed to help individuals understand and improve their mental well-being. Topics covered include therapy, relationships, parenting, stress management, and personal growth. Additionally, Psychology Today offers a directory of therapists, psychiatrists, and treatment centers to connect users with mental health professionals.

Visit website

Campaign Against Living Miserably : CALM provides support to men in the UK who are struggling with mental health issues. The organization offers a helpline and web chat services to assist those in need, aiming to reduce the stigma around mental health and prevent male suicide. CALM also engages in community outreach and awareness campaigns to promote mental well-being and encourage open conversations about mental health. Additionally, the organization collaborates with various partners to create safe spaces and support networks for men, ensuring they have access to the resources and help they need.

Visit website

Andy's Man Club : Andy's Man Club is a men's suicide prevention charity in the UK, offering free-to-attend peer-to-peer support groups. They aim to end the stigma surrounding men's mental health and provide a safe space for men to talk. Meetings are held every Monday at 7 PM, except on bank holidays. Their mission is to help men through the power of conversation and support.
